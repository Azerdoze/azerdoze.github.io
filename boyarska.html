Boyarska

the Northern most region in the human kingdoms is known as Boyarska.

It is a land filled by dense coniferal forests and constrasting highly populated cities.

Wind and cold are part of the daily life here, as even in the summer, temperatures are very low and winds blow strong. This accompanied by short summers and long winters makes it a country only for the strong willed.

With Pine woods all over the region and big fields to run free, fauna has flourished here. Herds of mooses can be seen running in the wild, while bears can be spotted hunting trouts in rivers.

To the North lies the Great Unknown. To the south is the Ghobi steppes. The East is blocked by the Unending Mountains and the West and Southwest frontiers with Varsemia.

Here, a federation of strong minded individuals has made home. Calling themselves the Tvor, this three different nations have mastered the land and protect it with strong hearts.
