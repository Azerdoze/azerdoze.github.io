Radhaputni

South of the Chientzu high walls and the mountain scarps of Daiju, a swampy jungle exists known as the jungle of Radphaputni.

It is the smallest region in the human kingdoms.

Its jungle is heavily dense and the swamps mistakenly deep, with many adventurers lost their lives exploring it.

With a diverse ecosystem, it has a rich fauna and flora. Not only that but the region is known for being the only place where dinosaurs still roam.

Other than dinosaurs, exotic animals like pachyderms, manatee, tigers, komodo dragons, binturongs, tarsiers and gibbons are some of the unique fauna of the region.

Besides it's northern borders, it is the southest region in the human kingdoms and further South there is only the unending Narar.

On this carismatic region, one human tribe managed to face the jungle's adversities and transform it to their profit: The Meynari.
