Nations

All the Human nations, from the biggest empire to the smallest tribe, can be found here. Delve in, and soak in a rich and diversified setting. The nations are categorized by region, so to be easier for newcomers to identify each of the many nations.

Varsemia »

One of the biggest regions is Varsemia, located in the center of the continent. Hosting the a great deal of nations, the region is known as the most temperate climate.

In Varsemia live the proud nations of: the Cretian Empire; the Mercantile Kaastian Republic; the family structured Rubran Kingdom; the nightly beveritan war tribes; the warmongering gernian tribes; and the confederate nations of the old Lampa Empire;

Najha »

To the left of Varsemia is a huge desert that thrives with life due to a major river and some oasis. This is Najha.

In it live the cunning nations of: The Muabin Empire; The Seph'terak monarchy; the slaver city-states of Merys and Lonys; The newborn oligarchy of Barcagenia; The golden Armatian Kingdom; And the Enigmatic Mukulu Tribes.

The northern continent is divided into to massive regions: Istid and Boyarska.

Istid »

Istid is home to the proud Krin clans and the big game hunters of the Tatamak tribes.

The region is a cold place. With small rivers breaking the boredom of forest and snow.

To the west, lies the coast, where the Tatamak live and make a living as fishermen. It's coast is filled with patches of ice covering the ocean.

It's northern part consists of jagged mountains, filling the place with danger and a long valley known as Shaulruk, that leads to the outer region of the "Great Unknown".

Mukulus »

Boyarska is the place where the mighty Tvor Federation lives. A cold place, brimming with dense forests and sturdy townfolk, the place is known for it's rich gold deposits.

Nakolota »

West of Varsemia lies a long prairie mixed with a rocky desert. This is the calm plains of Nakolota, a place where civilization has yet to touch. Here live the Maoqui shaman tribes and the Borigua pacifist society.

A place where the survival of the fittest applies, the rocky desert has a rich environment with a diverse and unique fauna to the continent.

Quetza »

South of the rocky desert of Nakolota and passing a narrow strait West of Varsemia, Lies the densily forested peninsula of Quetza.

A place where nature is queen, the peninsula is filled with a swampy jungle where large reptiles and felines live.

The place is an exotic environment, where the heat and humidity tend to be welcomed by the immense flora that lies here.

Here, after an intense civil war, the once mighty empire of the Quepa-qual crumbled, and from it's ashes three small kingdoms rose.

From the ruins of the ancient empire blossomed the still imperialist Aztenia, that make their home in the center of the peninsula.

The northern region is controlled by the industrialists of the Huayna Teocracy.

Lastly, the southern region is governed by the farmers of the old empire, now known as the Pacalia kingdom.
