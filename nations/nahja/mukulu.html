Assegai Exotic One Handed, Pierce, 1d6, x3, 30ft range, 5 lbs.
The White Rhino's Resistance Mukulu Characters start with Endurance and Diehard as bonus feats at level 1.
Savanna's Rest A mukulu only needs four hours of sleep to be considered well rested.
Cheetah's Mobility A Mukulu with light or no armor adds +10ft to his mobility.
Defenders of Humanity A mukulu character gets +2 to damage rolls against any non-human or non-animal enemy.
Oral Traditions Mukulu characters start illiterate. In order to know how to read and write, they need to spend 2 skill points upon level up or level 1.
Alignment Any Non-Chaotic
Proficiencies Lionskull Club, Iklwa, Iwisa, Isihlangu, Assegai.
Restrictions Cannot use Medium or heavy armor, with the exception of Hide Armor.
