Playable Classes

Name Inspiration Playable Classes
Moaqui Native American Tribes Fighter, Barbarian, Ranger, Hunter, Druid, Witch, Oracle, Shaman, Slayer.

Traits

Name Description
Masters of the Plains +2 to Knowledge (nature) and Handle Animal skill checks.
Legendary Axe Wielders +2 to Attack rolls with weapons from the Axe group.
Art of the Tomahawk Throwing Axes used by a Maoqui have a crit multiplier of x3, deal +2 to damage and have a range of 20ft.
Uncanny Survivors +2 to Fortitude and Reflex Saves.
Songs of Earth and Wind +2 to ECL of any spell with Earth or Air subtype and any spell that affects visibility.
Oral Traditions Moaqui characters start illiterate. In order to know how to read and write, they need to spend 2 skill points upon level up or level 1.
Restrictions Moaqui cannot use medium or heavy armor, with the exception of hide armor.
Proficiency Handaxe, Battleaxe, Throwing Axe, Greataxe, Spear, Shortbow.

Tatanka - Bison Tribe +2 to will saves. +2 to attack and damage rolls while charging. Animal Companion is Bison. Bison ECL is +2.
Wanbli - Eagle Tribe Alertness as bonus feat. +2 to Initiative Checks. Animal Companion is Eagle. Eagle ECL is +2.
Matho - Bear Tribe +2 to Intimidate checks. +2 Hit Points per level. +2 to Grapple checks. Animal Companion must be Bear (grizzly). Bear ECL is +2.
Manitu - Wolf Tribe +2 to Survival checks. +2 to Trip checks. Animal Companion must be Wolf. Wolf ECL is +2.
Zuzeca - Rattlesnake Tribe +2 to Stealth checks. Poison use as extra feat. +2 damage with daggers. Animal Companion must be Viper. Viper ECL is +2.
Igmuwatogla - Puma Tribe +2 to Jump checks. Ignore rocky difficult terrain. +1 to AC while dual wielding. Animal Companion must be Mountain Lion and has +2 ECL.
Hinhan - Owl Tribe + 2 to Sense Motive checks. Add one extra language. Familiar is Owl. Familiar ECL is +2.
Khangi - Crow Tribe +2 to Bluff checks. +1 ECL to arcane spells. Familiar must be Crow. Familiar ECL is +2.
Tashunke - Horse Tribe +2 to Ride checks. +10ft to move speed if light loaded. Animal Companion must be Horse. Horse ECL is +2.
